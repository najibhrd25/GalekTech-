# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

VentureDen is a portfolio project maintained by [Aayush Bharti](https://github.com/AayushBharti). Contributions, feedback, and discussions are welcome.

Please be respectful and constructive in all interactions — whether opening issues, submitting PRs, or participating in discussions.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate
- Provide constructive feedback
- Accept differing viewpoints gracefully

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Any conduct inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Unacceptable behavior can be reported to the project maintainer. All reports will be reviewed and addressed appropriately.
